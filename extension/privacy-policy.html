Dealink does NOT collect, store, or sell any personal data. Your browsing activity stays on your device.

What Dealink Does

Dealink is a browser extension that helps you find better prices for products you're already looking at online. When you visit a product page on a supported store, Dealink extracts the product name and price to search for better deals across other retailers.

What Data We Access

  • Product information only: Product title, price, brand, model, and product identifiers (UPC/GTIN/ASIN) from product pages you visit on supported stores (Amazon, eBay, Walmart, Best Buy, Target, Newegg, AliExpress, KSP).
  • No personal data: We do NOT access your name, email, address, payment information, browsing history, or any data outside of product pages.
  • No tracking: We do NOT track which websites you visit, what you buy, or your browsing patterns.

How We Use Product Data

  • Product information is sent to our backend server only to search for matching products at lower prices on other stores.
  • Search results are cached temporarily (up to 2 hours) to improve performance and reduce API calls.
  • Product data is not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Product data is not shared with any third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Shopping (via SerpApi): We use Google Shopping's price comparison data to find deals. Product search queries are sent to this service. No personal information is included.
  • Google Favicons API: Used to display store logos in the extension popup. No personal data is sent.

Local Storage

  • Dealink uses chrome.storage.local to cache search results on your device for faster loading.
  • Cached data automatically expires after 2 hours.
  • You can clear all cached data by removing the extension or clearing extension storage in Chrome settings.

Anonymous Analytics

  • Dealink may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., "how many products were scanned", "average savings found").
  • These statistics contain no personally identifiable information.
  • Analytics can be disabled in the extension settings.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab: Access the current tab to extract product information.
  • storage: Cache search results locally for performance.
  • tabs: Detect when you navigate to a product page.
  • alarms: Periodically clean up expired cache data.
  • Host permissions (specific stores): Only access product pages on supported retailer websites.

Data Security

  • All communication between the extension and our server uses HTTPS encryption.
  • No data is stored permanently on our servers.
  • We follow security best practices for API key management and server configuration.

Your Rights

  • You can uninstall Dealink at any time to stop all data processing.
